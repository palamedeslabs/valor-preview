Honest about fit

Who Valor is,
and who it isn't for.

Coaching only works when both sides are aligned on what we're actually doing. Read this honestly before applying. There's no hard feelings if it isn't a fit, but there's no point pretending it is.

This is for you if

  • You compete (junior, college, amateur, or pro) and your scores matter
  • You're committed to 2–4 sessions a week and the work between them
  • You want measurable speed and strength gains tied to your game
  • You can train in person at Crull Fitness in Richardson, TX
  • You take coaching seriously and want a real partnership

This isn't for you if

  • You're looking for a one-off session or a casual gym membership
  • You're shopping primarily on price, not fit
  • You expect distance gains without the consistency to earn them
  • You'd rather follow a generic program than be coached individually
